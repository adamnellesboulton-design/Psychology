The mind competes for one channel of attention, and a regulator decides what gets through. Pick a condition to see all of its graphs at once -- why mania swings, why psychosis fragments and sticks, why ADHD and autism hold one fixed shape. Runs entirely in your browser.

A deliberately simple toy for the theory, not a fit to data. Under the hood it is one equation run with different settings; for the dynamicists, the two malfunctions are a fold (schizophrenia) and a Hopf bifurcation (bipolar).
