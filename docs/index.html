Malfunctions two faults x depth

Two faults, located by depth: set on the viewed level (drill with the layer picker above). Division gain G at the top → schizophrenia, at n-1 → autism. Fill damping kP at the top → bipolar, at n-1 → ADHD. A lesion deeper than n-1 is absorbed before it reaches the top, so it never expresses.

Environment volatility & tilt

Chaos is the environment's volatility — the amplitude of the always-on noise on the fill and on the world reading. 1x is the calibrated baseline; turn it up to see how a turbulent environment stresses each regime's stability. Feast vs famine is the tilt on that environment: feast (+) lifts the fill (manic), famine (-) lowers it (depressed), 0 is neutral. Mood tracks the fill; bipolar swings between the two poles.
